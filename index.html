Qur'an Recitation Maqāmāt We Teach

Qur'an recitation maqāmāt are melodic mode families that shape tone and emotional color while Tajweed and Quranic words remain unchanged.

  • Bayātī: Warm and soft for beginnings and humility.
  • Rast: Balanced and clear for stable, general recitation.
  • Ḥijāz: Distinctive and intense for awe and longing.
  • Nahāwand: Reflective tone for contemplation and supplication.
  • Ṣabā: Deeply plaintive for warning verses and heartfelt duʿā’.
  • Sīkāh: Intimate and tender for soulful pleading passages.
  • Jihārkāh: Bright and expansive for uplifting emphatic ayat.
  • Kurd: Straightforward, serious, and calm expression.
