About our academy

Built Around Tajweed Precision and Maqamat Beauty

We are an online Quran academy serving English-speaking students in the USA, UAE, and other countries with qualified teachers whose core specialty is Tajweed and Qur'an recitation maqāmāt.

Start Learning View Courses

Our Mission

Our mission is to make authentic, high-quality Quran education centered on precise Tajweed and beautiful maqāmāt recitation, delivered through caring teachers, clear lesson plans, and consistent mentorship.

What We Teach

Tajweed & Maqamat

Structured guidance in Bayātī, Rast, Ḥijāz, Nahāwand, Ṣabā, Sīkāh, Jihārkāh, and Kurd while preserving Tajweed and Qur'anic words.

Qaida & Nazira

Foundational reading support through Noorani Qaida, pronunciation correction, and fluency-focused Nazira practice.

Hifz & Revision

Personalized memorization plans with regular muraja'ah and progress checkpoints to keep students consistent.

Translation & Understanding

English-focused Quran translation lessons to connect recitation with meaning and practical reflection.

Who We Serve

Our programs are designed for children, teens, and adults — from complete beginners to advanced reciters seeking refinement.

  • Qualified male and female Quran teachers
  • Flexible schedules for US and global time zones
  • One-on-one and small-group online sessions
  • Supportive class environment for every age group
