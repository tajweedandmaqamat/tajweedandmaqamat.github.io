About Tajweed & Maqamat Academy

We are an online Quran academy serving English-speaking students in the USA, UAE, and other countries with qualified teachers whose core specialty is Tajweed and Qur'an recitation maqāmāt.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make authentic, high-quality Quran education centered on precise Tajweed and beautiful maqāmāt recitation, delivered through caring teachers, clear lesson plans, and consistent mentorship.

Our Main Learning Focus

Qur'an recitation maqāmāt are melodic mode families that shape tone and emotional color in recitation while keeping Tajweed and the words unchanged. We provide structured training in:

  • Bayātī (warm, soft, and familiar)
  • Rast (balanced, upright, and clear)
  • Ḥijāz (awe-filled and spiritually intense)
  • Nahāwand (reflective and contemplative)
  • Ṣabā (plaintive and heartfelt)
  • Sīkāh (intimate and tender)
  • Jihārkāh (expansive and bright)
  • Kurd (simple, serious, and calm)

Other Courses We Offer

  • Qaida and Nazira for foundational reading fluency
  • Hifz and revision plans with accountability
  • Quran translation and understanding in English
  • Flexible online schedules for US and international families

Who We Serve

Our programs are designed for children, teens, and adults — from complete beginners to advanced reciters seeking refinement.
