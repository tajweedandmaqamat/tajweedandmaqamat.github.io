About Tajweed & Maqamat Academy

We are an online Quran academy serving English-speaking students in the USA, UAE, and other countries with qualified Quran teachers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make authentic, high-quality Quran education accessible through caring teachers, clear lesson plans, and consistent mentorship.

What Makes Us Different

  • Qualified teachers with Ijazah and teaching experience
  • Male and female Quran teachers, including female teachers for sisters
  • Courses in Qaida, Nazira, Hifz, Tajweed, and Quran translation
  • Flexible online schedules for US and international families
